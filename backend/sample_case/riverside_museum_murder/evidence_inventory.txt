NOTES:

Exhibit 1-A (bookend) is the primary suspect weapon. Its displacement from
Bellweather's office to Gallery 7 — where the body was found — is unexplained
and materially significant. The bookend was a matched pair allocated exclusively
to Bellweather's office per museum inventory records.

The financial discrepancies identified in Exhibits 5, 7, and 11 appear consistent
with procurement fraud, estimated total discrepancy of approximately £58,000–85,000
if all three acquisition lines are confirmed fraudulent. The victim was in the
process of documenting and escalating this finding at the time of her death.
