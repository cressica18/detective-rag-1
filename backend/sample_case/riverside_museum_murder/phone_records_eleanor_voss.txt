NOTES FROM INVESTIGATING OFFICER — Det. L. Harte, March 17, 2024:

1. Unregistered number (714) 555-0822: This number appears repeatedly in
   Voss's call records in the days before her death. The number is a prepaid
   mobile, subscriber details not on file. Investigation into identity of
   this number is ongoing.

2. March 15, 06:58 PM call to Dr. Amelia Voss (sister): This 2-minute 17-second
   call to her sister is the last confirmed completed call made by Eleanor Voss.
   Content unknown. Dr. Amelia Voss has provided a statement (see witness
   statement file) indicating Eleanor called to say she was "running late"
   for their usual Friday dinner and would try to leave work by 7:30 PM.

3. Missed calls from Dr. Amelia Voss at 7:02 PM, 7:31 PM, and 7:45 PM:
   Eleanor Voss did not answer these calls. This is consistent with time-of-death
   estimates placing her death between 7:45 PM and 8:30 PM — she may have
   already been incapacitated or deceased by the time of the 7:45 PM call.
   (The 7:02 PM missed call could indicate she was occupied, distressed, or
   confronted — this is consistent with a confrontation beginning shortly after
   her 6:58 PM call to her sister.)

4. March 15, 03:44 PM call to Dr. Amelia Voss (8m 51s): Dr. Amelia Voss has
   stated in her witness statement that during this call, Eleanor mentioned
   she had "found something troubling" at work and was deciding how to handle it.
   She did not specify what or who was involved during this call. This is
   consistent with the audit document found on Eleanor Voss's laptop.

5. The lack of any call to museum HR, police, or any authority on March 15
   suggests Eleanor had not yet formally escalated her concerns before her death.
