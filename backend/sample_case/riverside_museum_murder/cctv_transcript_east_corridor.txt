NOTES FROM REVIEWING OFFICER — DFO P. HUANG:

The male figure observed entering and exiting Gallery 7 between 20:17:31 and
20:21:44 was subject to enhanced frame analysis. The following observations
are noted:

1. The figure's height (estimated 1.80–1.83m based on corridor reference
   markings) is consistent with Marcus Bellweather as recorded in HR records
   (1.82m).

2. The figure's dark jacket and briefcase are consistent with clothing described
   by the museum receptionist as worn by Bellweather earlier that day.

3. At 20:17:22, a partial frontal view lasting approximately 1.2 seconds was
   captured. Facial recognition analysis (conducted via RPD DigitalID v3.2)
   returned a 74% probability match to Marcus Bellweather from his employee
   ID photograph. This probability score is below the 85% threshold for
   definitive identification and should be treated as PROBABLE, NOT CONFIRMED,
   for evidentiary purposes without corroborating evidence.

4. The figure's entry into Gallery 7 at 20:17:31 and exit at 20:21:44
   (approximately four minutes inside the gallery) coincides with the estimated
   time-of-death window established in the forensic and autopsy reports
   (estimated death: 19:45 PM – 20:30 PM).

5. The elapsed time between the figure's exit from the corridor frame
   (20:21:52) and the badge-exit record for Marcus Bellweather at the
   Mercer Street staff exit (20:22:34) is approximately 42 seconds — consistent
   with the distance from the east corridor stairwell to the Mercer Street exit.
